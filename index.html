About DFRN

We are a group of researchers, architects and technologists coming together to design and create the infrastructure for decentralised scientific practices around cementitious and concrete 3D Printing in architecture and engineering. We are exploring the potential of a decentralised - (blockchain governed) - physical infrastructure for the production of a building kit-of-parts using 3D printing and additive manufacturing
